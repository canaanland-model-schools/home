📖 KEY VOCABULARY (Listen carefully)

Equation: A mathematical sentence with an equals sign (=). Example: x + 5 = 12.

Expression: Math phrase without equals sign. Example: 3x + 5.

Variable: The unknown value, usually a letter like x, y, m.

Solution: The value that makes the equation true.

LHS and RHS: Left-hand side and right-hand side of the equals sign.

⭐ GOLDEN RULE OF SOLVING EQUATIONS

🎤 TEACHER: Whatever you do to one side, you MUST do to the other side. Use inverse operations (opposite operations) to find the variable.

Addition ↔️ Subtraction   |   Multiplication ↔️ Division

🔧 CASE 1: ADDITION EQUATIONS (Subtract same number)

Example 1: x + 6 = 14 → Subtract 6 from both sides → x = 8. Check: 8+6=14 ✅

Example 2: y + 9 = 20 → Subtract 9 → y = 11 ✅

Example 3: m + 15 = 30 → Subtract 15 → m = 15 ✅

🔧 CASE 2: SUBTRACTION EQUATIONS (Add same number)

Example 1: x - 5 = 8 → Add 5 to both sides → x = 13. Check: 13-5=8 ✅

Example 2: y - 7 = 12 → Add 7 → y = 19 ✅

Example 3: m - 10 = 25 → Add 10 → m = 35 ✅

🔧 CASE 3: MULTIPLICATION EQUATIONS (Divide both sides)

Example 1: 4x = 20 → Divide by 4 → x = 5 ✅

Example 2: 7y = 42 → Divide by 7 → y = 6 ✅

Example 3: 9m = 63 → Divide by 9 → m = 7 ✅

🔧 CASE 4: DIVISION EQUATIONS (Multiply both sides)

Example 1: x/3 = 5 → Multiply by 3 → x = 15 ✅

Example 2: y/4 = 7 → Multiply by 4 → y = 28 ✅

Example 3: m/5 = 9 → Multiply by 5 → m = 45 ✅

🚀 TWO-STEP EQUATIONS (Undo +/- first, then ×/÷)

Example 1: 2x + 5 = 13 → Subtract 5 → 2x = 8 → Divide by 2 → x = 4. Check: 2×4+5=13 ✅

Example 2: 3y - 7 = 14 → Add 7 → 3y = 21 → Divide by 3 → y = 7 ✅

Example 3: 5m + 8 = 33 → Subtract 8 → 5m = 25 → Divide by 5 → m = 5 ✅

Example with division: x/2 + 3 = 10 → Subtract 3 → x/2 = 7 → Multiply by 2 → x = 14 ✅

⚖️ VARIABLES ON BOTH SIDES

Example: 5x = 3x + 10 → Subtract 3x → 2x = 10 → Divide by 2 → x = 5. Check: 5×5 = 25, 3×5+10=25 ✅

Example 2: 7y - 4 = 5y + 6 → Subtract 5y → 2y - 4 = 6 → Add 4 → 2y = 10 → Divide by 2 → y = 5 ✅

📚 REAL-LIFE WORD PROBLEMS (Step-by-step)

Problem 1: A number increased by 7 gives 20. Let number = x → x+7=20 → x=13. The number is 13.

Problem 2: Three times a number is 42 → 3x=42 → x=14. The number is 14.

Problem 3: John is x years old. In 5 years he will be 18 → x+5=18 → x=13. John is 13 years old now.

Problem 4: A pen costs Nx. Five pens cost N75 → 5x=75 → x=15. One pen costs N15.

Problem 5: Mary spent N250, she has N150 left → m-250=150 → m=400. She had N400 at first.

📝 QUICK SUMMARY

Equation has = . Variable is unknown. Use inverse operations. Keep both sides balanced. Always check your answer. Word problems: Let x = unknown → equation → solve → sentence.
